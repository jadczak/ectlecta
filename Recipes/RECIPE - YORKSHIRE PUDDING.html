Sage and Cheese Yorkshire Pudding

2-24hr (20m)

Ingredients

  • 175g Milk
  • 150g AP flour
  • 75g Cheddar Cheese
  • 30g Danish Blue Cheese
  • 100ml (20 tsp) melted tallow
  • 2g Salt
  • 4g Sodium Citrate
  • 4 Eggs
  • 25g Water
  • 5g Sage

Tools

  • Immersion Blender
  • Blender
  • Oven
  • Muffin Pan

Notes:

STEP 1

Ingredients

  • 100g Whole Milk (100%)
  • 4g sodium citrate (4%)
  • 75g Cheddar (75%)
  • 30g Danish Blue (30%)

Tools

  • Immersion Blender

In a small sauce pot, whisk together 100g milk and 4g sodium citrate. Heat over medium heat until simmering

Prepare the cheese while the milk and sodium citrate heat. Shread, or chop into fine pieces, the cheddar and danish blue cheese.

When the milk and sodium citrate are simmering add 1/4 of the cheese mixture and blend with an immersion blender. Once the mixture is blended add remainign cheese in batches and blend until yolu have a smooth cheese sauce.

STEP 2

Ingredients

  • 75g Whole Milk
  • 150g AP Flour
  • 4 Eggs
  • Sauce from Step 1
  • 25g Water
  • 2g Salt
  • 5g Sage

Tools

  • Blender

Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth. place into airtight contianer and rest in refridgerator for 1 hour (or overnight)

STEP 3

Ingredients

  • 100ml (20 tsp) Beef Tallow

Tools

  • Oven
  • Muffin Tin

Preheat over to 450*f (add *c)

Add 10ml (2tsp) beef tallow to each muffin tin

When oven is heated add muffin tin with tallow to the oven and let heat for 10 minutes

STEP 4

Ingredients

  • Yorkshire Pudding Batter (from Step 2)

Tools

  • Muffin Tin

Remove preheated muffin tin from oven

Pour batter on top of heated tallow, filling each tin 2/3-3/4 full

Return muffin tin to oven and cook for 20 minutes, rotating pan at 10 minu8tes (half way) in.

STEP 5

Ingredients

  • Yorkshire Pudding

Tools

When the yorkshire pudding appears done (~20m) remove from oven serve immediately (ok really give it like 1 minute so they do not burn you or your guests)
